About JoveHeal

Our Mission

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the difference between coaching and therapy?
A: JoveHeal provides mindset coaching, not therapy. Our coaching focuses on goal-setting, mindset development, and personal growth. For mental health conditions, we recommend consulting with licensed mental health professionals.

Q: How long are the coaching programs?
A: Program lengths vary. The Balance Mastery+ program typically runs 8-12 weeks, while the Inner Mastery Lounge membership is ongoing with monthly subscription options.

Q: Are sessions available online?
A: Yes, we offer both in-person and online sessions to accommodate clients worldwide.

Q: What is the refund policy?
A: Please contact us directly for information about refunds and cancellation policies for specific programs.

Q: Can I join group sessions if I'm new to coaching?
A: Absolutely! Our group sessions welcome participants at all levels. Our facilitators create a supportive environment for everyone.
