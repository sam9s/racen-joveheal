My Story

For years, I thrived in the structured world of software engineering—leading teams, solving complex problems, and delivering high-impact results. But beneath the surface, I felt an undeniable misalignment. Despite my professional success, I battled stress, self-doubt, and a persistent longing for something deeper—something beyond deadlines and performance metrics.

Then life forced me to stop. Severe health challenges became my wake-up call. My body was screaming what my mind had ignored for years—the cost of chronic stress, overwork, and emotional suppression. At the same time, I found myself navigating painful relationship struggles, questioning my worth, boundaries, and ability to truly connect. These experiences shattered me, but in that breaking, I found something even more powerful—healing.

My breakthrough came when I discovered the transformative power of energy work, mindset shifts, and deep inner healing. What began as a personal journey quickly became a calling. I transitioned from engineering to coaching, blending my analytical expertise with intuitive wisdom to create a unique and results-driven approach.

What sets my work apart? I blend 20 years of corporate, leadership, Agile transformation, and powerful energy healing modalities like NLP, Theta Healing, Medical Intuition, and advanced mindset coaching. This fusion of strategy and energetics allows high achievers to break free from mental blocks, realign their energy, and step into their full power with clarity and confidence.
